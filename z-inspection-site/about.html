About Z-Inspection®

A global initiative for trustworthy, responsible, and mindful AI assessment

The Association

The Association for Responsible and Trustworthy AI: Z-Inspection® is a Swedish non-profit association (ideell förening) established in 2026. The association provides the organizational and legal framework for the Z-Inspection® initiative, which has been active since 2019.

Z-Inspection® is a holistic, general-purpose process for evaluating the trustworthiness of AI-based systems. It was developed by an international, interdisciplinary group of experts spanning ethics, law, technology, medicine, and domain-specific disciplines. The process is grounded in the EU Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI and the ALTAI (Assessment List for Trustworthy AI) framework.

Mission

To promote trustworthy, responsible, and mindful AI, including the protection of fundamental and human rights. The association achieves this through independent assessments, peer-reviewed research, education, and community building across sectors and regions.

Recognition

  • Listed in the OECD Catalogue of AI Tools and Metrics
  • Recipient of the ISSIP 2025 Award for the Dutch government AI assessment pilot
  • Peer-reviewed publications in IEEE Transactions on Technology and Society, Frontiers in Human Neuroscience, Springer, and other journals
  • Conferences organized in Venice (2023), Hamburg (2024), and Seoul (2025)
