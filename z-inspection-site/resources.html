Frameworks and Standards

The Z-Inspection® methodology builds on and aligns with the following frameworks and regulatory instruments.

EU Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI

Published by the High-Level Expert Group on AI (2019). Defines seven key requirements for trustworthy AI that form the foundation of Z-Inspection® assessments.

ALTAI

Assessment List for Trustworthy AI. A practical self-assessment checklist developed by the European Commission based on the Ethics Guidelines. Used as a structured tool within Z-Inspection®.

EU AI Act

Regulation 2024/1689. The world's first comprehensive AI regulation. Z-Inspection® supports compliance with its requirements for risk assessment, transparency, and human oversight.

OECD AI Principles

Z-Inspection® is listed in the OECD Catalogue of AI Tools and Metrics, recognizing it as a validated approach for trustworthy AI assessment.
