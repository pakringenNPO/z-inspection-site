The Process

A systematic approach to ethical AI assessment

Z-Inspection® uses socio-technical scenarios to identify ethical issues and tensions throughout the AI lifecycle, aligned with EU guidelines for trustworthy AI.

1. Set Up

Establish the scope, identify stakeholders, form the interdisciplinary assessment team, and verify preconditions for a trustworthy AI assessment.

2. Assess

Map socio-technical scenarios, identify ethical tensions using the ALTAI framework, and analyze conflicts between principles and stakeholder interests.

3. Resolve

Address identified tensions through structured dialogue, propose actionable recommendations, and establish ongoing monitoring and maintenance protocols.
